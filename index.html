About

    I am currently a Research Scientist at Google. I received my Ph.D. from Stanford University, advised by Professor Leonidas J. Guibas. Prior to joining Stanford, I got my bachelor degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University.
    My recent research interests focus on 3D perception and shape analysis, with the goal of equipping robotic agent with the ability of understanding and interacting with the 3D world. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research or potential collaborations.
